About

Jessica is a freelance web developer/consultant with 5+ years experience building custom themes on the Shopify Platform.

With an eye for design and a passion for debugging, Jessica strives to build intuitive and engaging e-commerce experiences for all users, regardless of how they access the web. Her knowledge of HTML, CSS, Liquid, JavaScript, and Ruby empowers her to turn designs into highly customized Shopify themes for clients.
